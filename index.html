Frequently Asked Questions

How do I vote in the election?

You can vote either online through the student portal or in person at designated polling stations across campus. Make sure to bring your student ID.

When will voting take place?

Voting will be open from 8AM to 5PM on November 15th, 2023. Results will be announced the following day.

Can I meet the candidates in person?

Yes! Candidates will be holding meet-and-greet sessions throughout the campaign period. Check the events calendar for specific times and locations.

How long is the term for elected officials?

Scholar Council members serve for one academic year, from the inauguration ceremony until the next election season.
